# Time Dilation

The slowing of time at high speeds or in gravity.

## Key Facts

- Time passes differently depending on speed and gravity
- If you move fast enough, your time slows relative to others
- This is time travel into the future
- You've already experienced this — just slightly

## Why It Matters

Time dilation is the mechanism by which you can travel into the future. By moving fast enough, you can experience a few years while centuries pass elsewhere.
