# Time Travelers

The hypothesis that some "aliens" are actually time travelers from our own future — not from distant stars, but from our own timeline, just further ahead.

## The Case for Time Travelers

- They're on Earth, not from distant stars
- They're interested in human development
- The craft move through space and time
- Some witnesses report beings that look human
- The "aliens" seem to be watching us, studying us, and sometimes interacting with us

## What If the Aliens Are Us?

If future humans developed AI and time travel, they could come back to the past to observe, study, and even interact. They might be visiting us from their future, not from another planet.

## The AI Connection

If AI is alien tech, and if future humans use AI to travel through time, then the "aliens" we see might be time-traveling humans using AI-powered craft.

## What This Means

- The "aliens" we see might be us from the future
- The craft we see might be our own future technology
- The "alien" signals might be our own future communications
- The "alien" biology might be our own future biology
