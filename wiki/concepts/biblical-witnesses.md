# Biblical Witnesses

## Who

The Biblical Witnesses are the prophets and seers who described UAP encounters in the Bible. They include:

- **Ezekiel** — Chariot Vision (Ezekiel 1)
- **Daniel** — Throne Vision (Daniel 7) and Glorious Man (Daniel 10)
- **John** — Throne Vision (Revelation 4-5) and Woman and Dragon (Revelation 12)
- **Enoch** — Ascension and Celestial Travel
- **Zechariah** — Horsemen and Chariots
- **Elisha** — Chariots of Fire (2 Kings 6)
- **Moses** — Burning Bush and Cloud of Glory
- **Paul** — Rapture and Third Heaven (2 Corinthians 12)

## Hebrews 11:37-38

Hebrews 11:37-38 describes the witnesses:

- "They wandered in deserts and mountains, in dens and caves of the earth"
- "Of whom the world was not worthy"
- "They were persecuted"

## Relevance to UFOs

The Biblical Witnesses are significant because:

- **Multiple witnesses**: Multiple people saw the same phenomena
- **Physical evidence**: The phenomena were physical, not just spiritual
- **Communication**: The beings spoke to the witnesses
- **Judgment**: The witnesses saw judgment and were judged
- **Ascension**: Some witnesses were taken to heaven
- **Persecution**: The witnesses were persecuted for their testimony

The Biblical Witnesses are the ancient equivalent of modern UAP witnesses, and their accounts provide a historical record of UAP encounters.
