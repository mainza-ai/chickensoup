# Bible UFO Testimonies

## Overview

The Bible contains numerous descriptions of UAP encounters, including:

1. **Ezekiel 1** — The Chariot Vision (c. 593 BC)
2. **Genesis 6:1-4** — The Nephilim (children of the gods)
3. **Daniel 7** — The Throne Vision
4. **Daniel 10** — The Glorious Man
5. **Revelation 4-5** — John's Throne Vision
6. **Revelation 12** — The Woman and the Dragon
7. **Hebrews 11:37-38** — The Witnesses
8. **2 Kings 6:15-17** — Elisha's Chariots of Fire
9. **Zechariah 1:8-17** — The Horsemen
