# Quantum Field Theory

The theoretical framework for quantum fields.

## Key Facts

- Quantum fields are the fundamental building blocks of the universe
- Quantum field theory describes how particles interact through fields
- Quantum field theory is the foundation of quantum computation

## Why It Matters

Quantum field theory is the theoretical framework for the time travel machinery. It describes how the quantum field of spacetime works.
