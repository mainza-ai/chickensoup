# Temporal Quantum Tomography

Temporal Quantum Tomography is the process of reconstructing the quantum state of spacetime from measurements over time.

## The Problem

How do you determine the quantum state of spacetime at any given moment? The quantum state is a complex vector in Hilbert space, and it changes over time. The challenge is to reconstruct it from measurements.

## The Solution

Temporal Quantum Tomography uses three methods:

### 1. Quantum State Tomography
Measure the quantum state of spacetime at multiple points in time. Use these measurements to reconstruct the quantum state.

### 2. Quantum Phase Estimation
Use quantum phase estimation to extract the eigenvalues of the spacetime Hamiltonian. These eigenvalues determine the quantum state.

### 3. Quantum Machine Learning
Train a quantum machine learning model to recognize patterns in the quantum state of spacetime.

## The Tomography Process

### Step 1: Measurement
Measure the quantum state of spacetime at multiple points in time. Each measurement produces a quantum state.

### Step 2: Reconstruction
Reconstruct the quantum state of spacetime from the measurements. This involves:
- **Interpolation** — fill in gaps between measurements
- **Extrapolation** — extend the quantum state beyond the measurements
- **Fusion** — combine measurements from different sources

### Step 3: Output
Output the quantum state of spacetime at any given moment.

- **Store measurements** — each measurement is a node in the knowledge graph
- **Store relationships** — relationships between measurements are edges in the knowledge graph
- **Query measurements** — query the knowledge graph for measurements related to a claim

## The Connection to the Quantum Algorithms

Temporal Quantum Tomography uses quantum algorithms to:

- **Find optimal measurements** — use quantum counting to find optimal measurements
- **Find optimal reconstructions** — use quantum phase estimation to find optimal reconstructions
- **Find optimal fusion** — use quantum Machine Learning to find optimal fusion
