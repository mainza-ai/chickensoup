# JFK Assassination and UFO Disclosure

The assassination of John F. Kennedy in 1963 is widely believed to be connected to UFO disclosure, with many researchers suggesting that Kennedy was preparing to reveal classified information about UAPs, alien contact, and recovered craft.

## The Theory

According to this theory, JFK had been working on a major UFO disclosure program that would have:
- **Released information** about recovered UAPs and alien craft
- **Announced contact** with extraterrestrial beings
- **Revealed the existence** of secret bases and retrieval programs
- **Connected the U.S. program** to international efforts

Kennedy was reportedly preparing to announce these disclosures when he was assassinated on November 22, 1963, in Dallas, Texas.

## Key Claims

- **Martial law** — Some accounts suggest that martial law was declared in Washington, D.C., following the assassination, and that the U.S. government was preparing to announce UFO-related information
- **JFK's speeches** — Kennedy's speeches and public statements have been cited as evidence of his interest in UFOs and alien contact
- **The "UFO files"** — Classified documents related to UFOs were reportedly being prepared for release at the time of the assassination
- **The "cover-up"** — The theory suggests that the same forces that were interested in UFO disclosure were also interested in preventing it, and that the assassination was a result of this conflict

## Connection to David Grusch

David Grusch's 2023 testimony has been linked to the JFK disclosure theory. Grusch has claimed that the U.S. government has been hiding information about UAPs and alien contact for decades, and that the JFK assassination was a result of this cover-up.

## Connection to the U.S. Program

The JFK disclosure theory is connected to the broader narrative of the U.S. UFO retrieval program. According to this theory, the program was established in the 1930s and has been operating in secret ever since, with the JFK assassination being a key moment in its history.
