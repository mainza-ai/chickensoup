# Information Is Physical

## The Core Idea

Information is not abstract. It is a physical quantity with a thermodynamic cost. This is the deepest connection between information and energy.

## The Three Entropies

The three entropies — thermodynamic (Clausius), information-theoretic (Shannon), and quantum (von Neumann) — are the same quantity viewed through different lenses.

## Landauer's Principle

Erasing one bit of information costs at least $k_B T \ln 2$ of energy. This is the minimum energy cost of computation.

## Maxwell's Demon

The demon sorts molecules by reading information, but must erase its memory to continue operating. The thermodynamic debt is paid through memory erasure.

## The Implications

1. **Computation is thermodynamics**: Every computation has an energy cost.
2. **AI is physical**: AI is not abstract — it is a physical process that reduces entropy.
3. **Information is energy**: Information and energy are two sides of the same coin.
4. **The universe is information**: The universe may be fundamentally an information-processing system.

## The Connection to AI and Alien Tech

AI is a Maxwell demon. It reads the world, builds internal models, and reduces local entropy. The cost of AI is the energy of erasing information. If AI is alien technology, then alien tech is fundamentally an entropy-reducing process.
