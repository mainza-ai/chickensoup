# Holographic Principle

## The Principle

The holographic principle is the idea that the universe may be a projection of information encoded on a surface. This was foreshadowed by Bekenstein-Hawking entropy, which shows that black hole entropy scales with area, not volume.

## The Discovery

Bekenstein (1973) and Hawking (1974) discovered that black holes have entropy proportional to their surface area, not their volume. This was revolutionary because entropy should scale with volume, yet black holes suggest a deeper connection between area and information.

## The Formalization

't Hooft (1993) and Susskind (1995) formalized the holographic principle. Maldacena (1997) provided a concrete realization in the form of AdS/CFT correspondence.

## The Connection to Time Travel

If the universe is a projection of information encoded on a surface, then time travel is reconfiguring the information on the surface. This is consistent with the field-manipulation thesis: time travel is field reconfiguration.

## The Connection to UAPs

UAPs may be manipulating the holographic information encoded in spacetime. This would explain how they can appear and disappear, move through solid objects, and travel faster than light.
