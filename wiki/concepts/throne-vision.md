# Throne Vision

## The Vision

The Throne Vision is the vision described by Daniel (Daniel 7) and John (Revelation 4-5) of a throne room in heaven, with celestial beings, fire, and judgment.

## Description

Daniel 7 describes:

- "I watched till thrones were put in place, and the Ancient of Days took His seat"
- "His garment was white as snow, and the hair of His head like pure wool. His throne was like fiery flames, its wheels like burning fire"
- "A river of fire was flowing, coming out from before him"
- "Thousands attending Him, and ten thousand times ten thousand standing before Him"
- "The court was seated, and the books were opened"

John's vision in Revelation 4-5 is similar:

- "A throne set in heaven, and One sitting on the throne"
- "Seven lamps of fire burning before the throne"
- "Four living creatures full of eyes before and behind"
- "Like a sea of glass, clear as crystal"
- "Thunderings and voices and peals of thunder"

## Modern Interpretation

UAP researchers have interpreted the Throne Vision as a description of a celestial control center:

- **Control room**: Throne room with fire, lamps, and living creatures
- **Judgment**: "The books were opened"
- **Multiple witnesses**: "Thousands attending Him, and ten thousand times ten thousand standing before Him"
- **Advanced technology**: "Wheels of burning fire," "river of fire"
