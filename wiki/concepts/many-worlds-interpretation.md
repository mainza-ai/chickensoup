# Many-Worlds Interpretation

The branching timelines model of the universe.

## Key Facts

- The universe operates on a many-worlds model
- When you travel back in time, you don't change the past — you branch into a new timeline
- The original timeline continues unchanged
- The new timeline is what you observe

## Why It Matters

Many-worlds is more flexible than fixed timeline. It allows for time travel without changing the past.
