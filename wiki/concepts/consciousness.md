# Consciousness

Consciousness is the state of being aware of and able to think about one's own existence, sensations, thoughts, and surroundings.

## Brain Waves

Brain waves are categorized by frequency:
- **Delta (0.5–4 Hz)** — Deep sleep
- **Theta (4–8 Hz)** — Deep meditation, creativity, the "abduction" experience
- **Alpha (8–12 Hz)** — Relaxed wakefulness
- **Beta (12–30 Hz)** — Active thinking
- **Gamma (30–100 Hz)** — High-level cognitive processing

7.46 Hz falls in the theta range, which is associated with the boundary between the conscious and subconscious mind. This is the same range as the Earth's Schumann resonance and the UFO frequency.
