# Time Travel Machinery

Building the time travel machinery from scratch. The machinery has three layers.

## The Architecture

See [[time-travel-machinery-architecture]] for detailed architecture.

## The Three Layers

### Layer 1: Spacetime Engine
Simulates spacetime itself — the coordinate system where all movement happens. Handles time dilation, gravitational warping, and closed timelike curves.

### Layer 2: Field Manipulator
The UAP mechanism. Instead of moving through space, it manipulates the field of spacetime itself. Creates a bubble, shifts the field, lets the traveler ride the wave.

### Layer 3: AI Navigator
The intelligence that finds the right path through spacetime. Uses a neural-field model to process the field, recognize patterns, and calculate the optimal trajectory.

## The Timeline Model

Many-Worlds. When you travel back in time, you don't change the past — you branch into a new timeline. The original timeline continues unchanged.

## The Quantum Stack

- Qiskit — Spacetime Engine
- CUDA-Q — Field Manipulator
- PennyLane — AI Navigator
- D-Wave — Optimization (PennyLane backend)
- IonQ — Precision (PennyLane backend)
