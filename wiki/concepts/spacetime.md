# Spacetime

The four-dimensional fabric of space and time.

## Key Facts

- Spacetime is not fixed — it bends, curves, and warps
- Gravity is just spacetime in motion
- Energy and matter are equivalent (E = mc²)
- The speed of light is the limit

## Why It Matters

If you can manipulate gravity, you're manipulating spacetime. If you can manipulate spacetime, you can manipulate time itself.
