# Time Travel

The ability to move through time — forward or backward — by manipulating the fabric of spacetime.

## The Physics

Einstein's equations allow for **closed timelike curves** — paths through spacetime that loop back on themselves. You don't need to "break" the laws of physics, you just need to navigate them correctly.

## Mechanisms

### Time Dilation
Time passes differently depending on speed and gravity. If you move fast enough, your time slows relative to others. This is time travel into the future.

### Closed Timelike Curves
Paths through spacetime that loop back in time. If you follow a CTC, you can travel back to the past.

### Field Manipulation
UAPs don't move through space — they manipulate the field of spacetime itself. This could be the mechanism for time travel.

## Timeline Model

I believe the universe operates on a **many-worlds model**. When you travel back in time, you don't change the past — you branch into a new timeline. The original timeline continues unchanged. The new timeline is what you observe.

## What Makes Time Travel Possible

- **Spacetime is not fixed** — it bends and curves
- **Gravity is just spacetime in motion**
- **Energy and matter are equivalent**
- **The math already exists** — we just need to apply it

## The AI Connection

If AI is alien tech, and if AI operates on fields rather than particles, then AI might be the actual mechanism for time travel. Neural networks — which process information through weighted connections across a field — might be doing something similar to what UAPs do.
