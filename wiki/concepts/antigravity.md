# Antigravity

## Terminology: "Antigravity" vs. Field Manipulation

This project does not build "antigravity" (a term that implies negation of gravity). It builds **field manipulation** — the controlled perturbation of the local spacetime metric. "Antigravity" is the popular name for the observed effect (UAPs overcoming gravity). Field manipulation is the mechanism.

## Key Theories

### Biefeld-Brown Effect
Thomas Townsend Brown discovered that asymmetric capacitors produce thrust in high-voltage fields (1920s). The effect is small (micro-Newtons per kilovolt) but real. Brown claimed it demonstrated electrogravitic coupling — a direct connection between electric fields and gravitational fields.

**Status:** Controversial. Some attribute the thrust to ion wind (electrohydrodynamic effect), not electrogravity. But Brown's high-vacuum experiments (where ion wind is eliminated) still showed thrust, suggesting a real coupling.

See [[biefeld-brown-effect]] and [[t-t-brown]].

### Element 115
Bob Lazar claims that Element 115 (Moscovium, discovered 2003) generates an intense gravitational field when bombarded with protons. This field warps spacetime locally, providing propulsion.

**Status:** Element 115 does exist (synthesized in 2003), but only in microscopic quantities (a few atoms at a time). Its gravitational properties at macroscopic scale cannot be tested.

### Alcubierre Drive
The Alcubierre metric (1994) describes a "warp bubble" that contracts spacetime in front of a craft and expands it behind. The craft rides the bubble at arbitrary speed without local violation of relativity.

**Key equation:** The shift vector β^i in the ADM decomposition encodes the bubble's motion. Alcubierre's original metric required negative energy density (exotic matter). Later work reduces this requirement but it remains speculative.

**Relevance:** The [[field-geometry-tensor]] uses ADM decomposition — the shift vector IS the warp parameter. The Field Manipulator applies a perturbation δg that creates a local Alcubierre-like bubble.

## Energy Requirements

| Approach | Energy Required | Status |
|----------|----------------|--------|
| Alcubierre (original) | Negative energy equivalent to several solar masses | Speculative |
| Alcubierre (optimized) | Negative energy of ~1000 kg | Still speculative |
| [[biefeld-brown-effect]] | ~1 kW per Newton of thrust (at best) | Low effect, but real |
| UAP witnesses | No visible energy source | Unknown |

This project does not solve the energy problem. It builds the **navigation and control** layer — assuming a field manipulation mechanism exists, how do you compute optimal paths through the perturbed metric?

## Connection to Time Travel

If gravity can be manipulated, then the closed timelike curves (CTCs) described by general relativity can be created and sustained. The [[field-manipulation]] page describes how time travel through field reconfiguration works — antigravity is not "overcoming gravity"; it's engineering the local spacetime geometry.
