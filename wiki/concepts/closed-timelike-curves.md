# Closed Timelike Curves

Paths through spacetime that loop back on themselves.

## Key Facts

- Einstein's equations allow for closed timelike curves
- A closed timelike curve is a path through spacetime that loops back in time
- If you follow a closed timelike curve, you can travel back to the past
- Closed timelike curves are the mechanism by which time travel works

## Why It Matters

Closed timelike curves are the mechanism by which time travel works. They are the paths through spacetime that allow you to travel back in time.
