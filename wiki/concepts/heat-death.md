# Heat Death

## The End of the Universe

Heat death (the end) is the scenario in which the universe expands and black holes evaporate, and entropy approaches its maximum. The universe will become a cold, featureless sea of photons.

## The Timeline

1. **Current**: The universe is expanding, stars are burning, black holes are forming.
2. **Stellar era**: Stars burn out, galaxies disperse.
3. **Degenerate era**: White dwarfs, neutron stars, and black holes remain.
4. **Black hole era**: Black holes dominate, slowly evaporating via Hawking radiation.
5. **Dark era**: The universe is a cold, featureless sea of photons.

## The Entropy Budget

The observable universe contains about $10^{104}$ bits of information, mostly in supermassive black holes. The Big Bang had about $10^{88}$ bits — an extraordinarily low entropy state.

## The Connection to Time Travel

Heat death is the end of time. When entropy reaches maximum, time effectively stops — no more interesting processes can occur. If time travel is entropy reconfiguration, then heat death is the ultimate entropy reconfiguration.

## The Connection to UAPs

UAPs may be harnessing exotic matter (negative energy) to locally reverse entropy. If so, they are delaying heat death locally — a form of time travel.
