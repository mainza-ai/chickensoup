# Death Ray

## Tesla's "Teleforce"

The "death ray" — which Tesla called **teleforce** — was a charged particle beam weapon. Tesla claimed it was a defensive weapon that could destroy aircraft at hundreds of miles, and that it was fundamentally different from conventional weapons because it used no explosive, no propellant, and no chemical reaction.

### Technical Claims
| Parameter | Tesla's Claim | Notes |
|-----------|--------------|-------|
| Particle type | Charged particles (mercury or tungsten microparticles) | Electrostatic acceleration |
| Voltage | > 400 kV | Multi-stage accelerator |
| Beam current | Microamps (not amps) | Low current, high energy per particle |
| Range | Hundreds of miles | Line of sight over horizon via ionospheric reflection? |
| Precision | "Can be aimed with great precision" | Focused by electrostatic repulsion |
| Power source | Wireless energy transmission | Linked to Wardenclyffe |

### How It Worked (per Tesla's Description)
1. A vacuum chamber produces a stream of charged microparticles
2. The particles are accelerated through a multi-stage electrostatic field (> 400 kV)
3. The beam is focused by electrostatic repulsion (same-charge particles repel, creating a narrow beam)
4. The beam is directed at the target; particles impact with enough kinetic energy to destroy
5. Power comes from the wireless energy system (see [[wireless-energy]])

This is closer to a modern **linear particle accelerator** or **electrostatic accelerator** than to a laser.

## What Happened to the Papers

Tesla died on January 7, 1943, in Room 3327 of the New Yorker Hotel. Within 24 hours, the **FBI Alien Property Custodian** seized all his papers, notebooks, and equipment.

**FBI File 65-606-472** documents the seizure:
- 80 trunks of papers, notes, and equipment
- 100+ patents and pending applications
- The teleforce plans
- Wardenclyffe schematics

The official reason: "prevent the invention from falling into enemy hands during wartime." The unofficial theory: Tesla's work was already connected to the UFO retrieval program (see [[ufo-retrieval-program]]).

**Status of the documents:**
- Some were declassified and released to the Tesla Museum in Belgrade
- Some were classified and reportedly transferred to the UFO retrieval program
- Some may still be classified

## Connection to UAP Energy Weapons

Whistleblowers (Grusch, Lazar) describe UAPs with directed energy capabilities:
- **Disable electronics** — Consistent with HPM (high-power microwave)
- **Vaporize targets** — Consistent with charged particle beam (Tesla's design)
- **No visible beam** — Consistent with accelerated neutral particle beam

Tesla's teleforce was a charged particle beam operating at non-relativistic speeds. UAP energy weapons appear more advanced (higher energy, longer range, no visible flash), but the principle — directed energy via field manipulation — is the same.

## Distinction from the "Death Ray" Myth

Popular culture imagines a death ray as a beam that explodes targets with visible radiation (like a sci-fi laser). Tesla's actual design was:
- **Not a laser** — It's a particle beam, not a photon beam
- **Nearly invisible** — Charged particles in air produce faint blue glow, not a visible "ray"
- **Not instantaneous** — Particles travel at significant fraction of c, but not at c
- **Not a "ray" at all** — It's a stream of particles, not electromagnetic radiation

The term "death ray" was sensationalized by the press. Tesla called it "teleforce."
