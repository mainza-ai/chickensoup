# The Thing

Non-human biologics — living or preserved extraterrestrial bodies — allegedly recovered by the U.S. military.

## Grusch's Claim

David Grusch testified in 2023 that the U.S. has recovered non-human biologics. He calls it "the thing." These are allegedly stored at various classified facilities.

## Key Claims

- The biologics are **non-human** in origin
- They are **living or preserved**
- They are **stored at classified facilities**
- The recovery has been going on for **decades**
- The biologics are part of the **retrieval program**

## What We Know

- Grusch testified under oath
- His claims are specific and consistent
- His claims are backed by other witnesses
- The biologics are part of the retrieval program

## What's Uncertain

- Whether the biologics are truly non-human
- The full extent of the recovery
- The location of the biologics
- The status of the biologics (living or preserved)
