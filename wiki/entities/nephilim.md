# Nephilim

## Who

The Nephilim were giants on earth in ancient times, described in Genesis 6:1-4 as the offspring of the "sons of God" and the "daughters of men."

## The Genesis Account

Genesis 6:1-4 describes:

- "The sons of God saw the daughters of men that they were beautiful; and they took wives for themselves of all whom they chose"
- "The Nephilim were on the earth in those days — and also afterward"
- "When the sons of God came in to the daughters of men, they bore children to them. Those were the mighty men who were of old, men of renown"
- The Nephilim were "heroes, who were of old, men of renown"

## Relevance to UFOs

The Nephilim are directly connected to the modern UAP narrative:

- **Non-human beings**: The "sons of God" are often interpreted as divine or extraterrestrial beings
- **Offspring**: They had children with human women, similar to modern reports of hybrid beings
- **Giants**: The Nephilim were physically large and powerful
- **Ancient origin**: They existed "in those days — and also afterward"
- **Mighty men**: They were "heroes" and "men of renown"

The Nephilim story is one of the earliest accounts of non-human beings interacting with humans, and it connects to the modern UAP narrative of non-human intelligences and their offspring.
