# Vatican UFO Crash Recovery

In 1937, the Vatican recovered a crashed UFO, which was later transferred to the United States.

## Timeline

- **1937** — A UFO crashes, recovered by the Vatican
- **Later** — The craft is transferred to the United States
- **Post-transfer** — The craft enters the U.S. UFO retrieval program, feeding into the same program that would recover craft at Roswell and beyond

## Significance

This recovery predates the commonly cited 1947 Roswell crash by a decade, supporting the claim that the UFO retrieval program may date to the 1930s. The Vatican's involvement adds a layer of historical and institutional credibility — the Vatican has extensive archives and resources for preserving rare objects and documents.

## Connection to Other Claims

- **Bob Lazar** — Claims the recovered craft used Element 115 for propulsion, suggesting the Vatican craft may have shared this technology
- **David Grusch** — Testified that hundreds of craft have been recovered, many of unknown origin
- **The Thing** — Non-human biologics allegedly recovered alongside craft, possibly including those from the Vatican crash
- **1930s origin** — Supports the theory that the retrieval program began in the 1930s, not just 1947
