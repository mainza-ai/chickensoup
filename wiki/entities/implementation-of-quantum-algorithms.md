# Implementation of Quantum Algorithms for Simulating Coupled Oscillators

**WISER Quantum Solutions Launchpad / Classiq. 2026.**

Concrete implementation of the theoretical algorithm from Babbush et al. (2023), providing executable quantum circuits for simulating coupled oscillators.

## Key Results

- Two implementations: end-to-end quantum and a simpler alternative
- Resource estimates for N=2 to N=16 (circuit width, depth, gate count)
- Confirms polylogarithmic scaling, supporting exponential speedup
- Two physical applications:
  - Extracting normal frequencies via Fourier transform
  - Computing wave propagation in the oscillator chain

## Relevance to UAP

The wave propagation and field dynamics simulated in this paper are directly applicable to understanding UAP field manipulation. The coarse-grained energy framework provides a natural model for how UAPs propagate through the spacetime field.
