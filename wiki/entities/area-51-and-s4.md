# Area 51 and S-4

Area 51 is a highly classified U.S. Air Force facility in Nevada. S-4 is a sub-base of Area 51 where alien craft are brought for repair and analysis.

## The Connection

- Bob Lazar claims that he worked at S-4, a sub-base of Area 51
- S-4 is where alien craft are brought for repair and analysis
- S-4 is located near Area 51
- S-4 is where the craft are kept
- S-4 is where the biologics are kept

## S-4 Features

- Located near Area 51
- Home of the alien craft
- Home of the biologics
- Home of the reverse-engineering program
- Home of the UAP research program

## What's Classified

- The full extent of UAP research at S-4
- The status of the craft
- The status of the biologics
- The status of the reverse-engineering program
