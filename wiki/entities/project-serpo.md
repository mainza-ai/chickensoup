# Project Serpo

Alleged secret program where 12 Americans were sent to Alpha Centauri in the 1950s and lived on a base there.

## Key Claims

- **12 Americans** were sent to Alpha Centauri
- The program began in the **1950s**
- The program ended in the **1980s**
- The Americans lived on a **base** on Alpha Centauri
- They sent back **coded radio signals**

## Evidence

- The program is alleged to be real
- The claims are consistent with other UAP claims
- The program is part of the retrieval program

## What's Uncertain

- The full extent of the program
- The status of the Americans
- The location of the base
- The status of the program
