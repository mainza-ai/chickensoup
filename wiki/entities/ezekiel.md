# Ezekiel

## Who

Ezekiel was a Jewish prophet who lived during the Babylonian exile (c. 593 BC). He was a priest before becoming a prophet.

## The Chariot Vision

Ezekiel's most famous vision (Ezekiel 1 and 10) describes a remarkable UAP encounter:

- A "whirlwind coming out of the north, a great cloud with raging fire engulfing itself"
- "Brightness was all around it and radiating out of its midst like the color of amber"
- Four living creatures with faces of a man, lion, ox, and eagle
- Each creature had four wings and hands under their wings
- Wheels "in the middle of a wheel" — described as having rims "full of eyes"
- The wheels were "like the color of beryl"
- The noise was "like the noise of many waters, like the voice of the Almighty, a tumult like the noise of an army"
- Ezekiel fell on his face when he heard the voice

This vision has been interpreted by UAP researchers as a detailed description of a metallic craft with lights, rapid movement, and multiple observers.

## Relevance to UFOs

Ezekiel's vision is one of the most detailed ancient descriptions of what appears to be a UAP encounter. The description includes:

- **Metallic appearance**: "color of amber," "color of beryl"
- **Lights**: "brightness was all around it," "eyes" on the wheels
- **Movement**: "lights going to and fro like the appearance of lightning"
- **Multiple observers**: Ezekiel was not alone
- **Physical effects**: Ezekiel fell on his face, was overwhelmed
