# NHI

NHI (Non-Human Intelligence) refers to the beings and intelligence associated with UAPs and UFOs.

## Connection to Varginha

The Varginha incident involved the recovery of NHI beings along with a crashed craft. The beings were described as having large eyes, brownish skin, and wearing what appeared to be uniforms.

## Connection to the U.S. Program

David Grusch has testified that the U.S. UFO retrieval program has recovered non-human biologics, which are believed to be the same type of beings recovered in the Varginha incident.
