# Qiskit

IBM's open-source quantum computing framework. The most mature quantum computing platform.

## Features

- **Qiskit Aer** — High-performance simulator for quantum circuits
- **Qiskit IBM Runtime** — Interface to IBM Quantum's cloud environment
- **Qiskit Serverless** — Long-running quantum jobs without manual configuration
- **Qiskit Ecosystem** — Collection of software and tutorials

## Why Qiskit for Time Travel

- Most mature ecosystem
- 1000+ qubits
- Qiskit Aer simulator is excellent
- Full toolchain: quantum circuits, operators, primitives, noise models
- Best for simulating quantum circuits that represent spacetime
