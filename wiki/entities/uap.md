# UAP (Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena)

The official term used by the U.S. military for UFOs. The U.S. Department of Defense switched from "UFO" to "UAP" in 2020 to reduce stigma and make the term more scientific.

## The Pentagon Connection

- Admiral John Richardson (Chief of Naval Operations) confirmed UAPs are real and cannot be ignored
- House Intelligence Committee hearing in 2023 — first public UAP hearing in decades
- The Pentagon has released multiple UAP videos (Gimbal, Goose, FFO)
- Aerial Combatting UAPs Task Force (ACUTAF) was established in 2020

## Key Claims

- The U.S. government has been studying UAPs for decades
- Some UAPs show advanced propulsion technology
- UAPs interact with military aircraft and weapons
- UAPs have been observed at nuclear facilities and military bases
- The retrieval program may have been running since the 1930s

## What's Classified

- Non-human biologics (if they exist)
- Full extent of the retrieval program
- Reverse-engineered craft
- The "black budget" for UAP programs (estimated tens of billions)
