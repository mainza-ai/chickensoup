# Enoch

## Who

Enoch was a descendant of Adam (Genesis 5:24) who "walked with God; then he was no more, because God took him." He is the only person in the Old Testament who did not die but was taken directly to heaven.

## The Book of Enoch

The Book of Enoch (1 Enoch) describes:

- Enoch's ascent through the heavens
- His vision of the celestial realm
- The watchmen (angels) who descended to earth
- The Nephilim and their origin
- The judgment of the wicked and the righteous

## Relevance to UFOs

Enoch's story is significant because:

- **Direct ascent**: He was "taken" to heaven, similar to modern abduction narratives
- **Celestial travel**: He traveled through the heavens and saw many things
- **The watchmen**: The angels who descended to earth are similar to modern "alien" beings
- **The Nephilim**: Enoch describes the offspring of the watchmen and human women
- **Judgment**: Enoch saw the judgment of the wicked and the righteous

Enoch's story is one of the earliest descriptions of a person who traveled to another realm and returned with knowledge.
