# Magenta UFO Crash

In 1933, a UFO crashed in the town of Magenta, Italy, and was recovered by Benito Mussolini's fascist regime.

## Timeline

- **1933** — UFO crashes in Magenta, Italy
- **1933–1937** — Recovered and studied under Mussolini's regime
- **~1937** — Often associated with the Vatican crash recovery (may be the same event or related)

## Key Claims

- **Location** — Magenta, a town in northern Italy near Milan
- **Recovery** — Recovered by Mussolini's regime, not the Vatican
- **Transfer** — After Italy's fall in the war, the craft may have been transferred to the United States
- **Connection to Vatican** — Often conflated with the 1937 Vatican crash recovery; may be the same event viewed from different perspectives, or two related recoveries in the same period

## Significance

This crash predates Roswell by 14 years and supports the theory that the UFO retrieval program began in the 1930s, possibly with multiple recoveries across Europe. Mussolini's regime had significant resources and scientific institutions capable of studying and preserving such craft.
