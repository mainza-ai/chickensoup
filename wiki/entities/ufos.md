# UFOs

Unidentified Flying Objects — the umbrella term for aerial phenomena that remain unexplained.

## Key Sightings

- **Roswell (1947)** — Mysterious craft crashed in New Mexico. Military initially said "flying disc," then retracted to "weather balloon."
- **Phoenix Lights (1997)** — Thousands saw a massive V-shaped craft. Governor Fife Symington confirmed sighting.
- **USS Nimitz (2004)** — Navy pilots recorded a Tic Tac-shaped object at impossible speeds. Videos declassified 2020.
- **The "Gimbal" (2015)** — Navy video showing UAP with unusual movement.
- **The "Goose" (2015)** — Navy video showing object moving from air to water.
- **FFO (2004)** — Fast Flying Object emerging from the sun.

## Characteristics

- Accelerate from 0 to Mach 10+ in seconds
- Move without plumes or sonic booms
- Cross between air and space seamlessly
- Hover motionless in the air
- Respond to radar and visual observation

## What We Know

- There are thousands of confirmed sightings with credible witnesses (pilots, military, astronauts)
- The U.S. government has publicly confirmed the existence of UAPs since 2020
- The Pentagon has released multiple videos of UAPs
- The military has been studying them for decades

## What's Uncertain

- Whether they are extraterrestrial, extraterrestrial, or something else
- Whether they are physical craft or other phenomena (plasma, gravitational effects)
- The full extent of the retrieval program
