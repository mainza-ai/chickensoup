# Ariel School UFO Incident

In September 1994, more than 60 schoolchildren at Ariel School in Ruwa, Zimbabwe, reported seeing a strange silver craft land near their playground. The children described seeing small beings with large eyes standing near the object, and some claimed the beings communicated with them through thought rather than speech.

## Key Details

- **Location** — Ariel School, Ruwa, Zimbabwe (near Harare)
- **Date** — September 1994
- **Witnesses** — Over 60 schoolchildren, aged 6–12
- **Description** — A silver, disc-shaped craft landed near the playground
- **The beings** — Small, with large eyes, standing near the craft
- **Communication** — The children reported telepathic communication with the beings
- **Consistency** — The children told their stories separately, yet described almost the same event

## Significance

- **Cross-cultural consistency** — The children were from diverse backgrounds and spoke different languages, yet their accounts were remarkably consistent
- **Age of witnesses** — The children were young enough that mass hysteria was considered less likely
- **Independent testimony** — The children were interviewed separately, reducing the possibility of contamination
- **Modern UFO incident** — One of the most talked-about UFO incidents in modern history, predating the recent wave of UAP attention

## Skepticism and Support

- **Skeptics** — Suggested mass hysteria or misinterpretation of ordinary phenomena
- **Supporters** — Researchers including Lyn Buchanan and Barbara Welz interviewed the children and found their accounts consistent and credible
- **Ongoing debate** — The Ariel School incident remains one of the most debated UFO sightings in the world

## Connection to Mount Nyangani

- **Lyn Buchanan** — Former CIA remote viewer who investigated the Ariel School incident and later claimed Mount Nyangani is one of four alien bases on Earth
- **Geographic proximity** — Mount Nyangani is located in Zimbabwe, near Ruwa where the Ariel School incident occurred
- **The being connection** — The beings described at Ariel School may be the same beings associated with the Mount Nyangani base
