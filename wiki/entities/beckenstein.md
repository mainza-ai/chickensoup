# Bekenstein

## Who

Jacob Bekenstein (born 1947) is an Israeli physicist who discovered that black holes have entropy proportional to their surface area.

## The Bekenstein Bound

The Bekenstein bound (1981) sets the maximum information in any region:

$$I \leq \frac{2\pi R E}{\hbar c \ln 2}$$

A black hole saturates this bound — it's the most information-dense object possible.

## The Discovery

Bekenstein showed that black holes have entropy proportional to their surface area, not their volume. This was revolutionary because entropy should scale with volume, yet black holes suggest a deeper connection between area and information.

## The Connection to Holography

This discovery foreshadowed the holographic principle: the universe may be a projection of information encoded on a surface.
