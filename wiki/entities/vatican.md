# Vatican

The headquarters of the Catholic Church in Rome, with extensive archives and a long history of preserving rare objects and documents.

## UFO Connection

- **1937** — The Vatican recovered a crashed UFO
- The craft was later transferred to the United States, entering the U.S. UFO retrieval program
- The Vatican's involvement adds institutional credibility to the UFO recovery narrative

## Archives

The Vatican maintains one of the world's most extensive archives, including:
- Ancient documents and manuscripts
- Scientific records
- Historical artifacts
- Possibly, records of the 1937 UFO recovery

## Significance

The Vatican's role in UFO recovery supports the theory that the retrieval program may date to the 1930s, predating the commonly cited Roswell crash. The Vatican's resources for preserving rare objects and its global influence make it a key player in the UFO narrative.
