# UFO Retrieval Program

The U.S. government's program for recovering and studying UFOs and UAPs.

## Timeline

- **1933** — Magenta crash in Italy, recovered by Mussolini's regime
- **1937** — Vatican recovers a crashed UFO (possibly related to or conflated with Magenta)
- **1930s** — Program begins (possibly earlier)
- **1947** — Roswell crash
- **1950s–1980s** — Project Serpo (alleged)
- **1989** — Bob Lazar claims work at S-4
- **2004** — USS Nimitz encounter
- **2017** — New York Times reports on UAP videos
- **2020** — Pentagon releases UAP videos, establishes ACUTAF
- **2023** — David Grusch testifies under oath

## Key Claims

- **Hundreds of craft recovered** — Grusch testified that the program has recovered hundreds of craft, not just a few
- **Non-human biologics** — Living or preserved extraterrestrial bodies, allegedly recovered
- **Reverse-engineering** — The military is building their own versions of UAP technology
- **Multi-branch program** — DIA, DARPA, NRO, CIA all involved
- **Black budget** — Estimated tens of billions in classified funding

## What We Know

- The program is real and has been running for decades
- Multiple agencies are involved
- The scale is larger than previously disclosed
- The craft are being studied and reverse-engineered

## What's Uncertain

- Whether all recovered craft are extraterrestrial
- Whether the biologics are non-human
- The full extent of the program
- The location of key facilities
