# John

## Who

John was one of the twelve apostles of Jesus and the author of the Gospel of John and the Book of Revelation.

## The Throne Vision

John's vision in Revelation 4-5 is remarkably similar to Ezekiel's:

- "A throne set in heaven, and One sitting on the throne"
- "Seven lamps of fire burning before the throne"
- "Four living creatures full of eyes before and behind"
- The creatures had faces of a lion, ox, man, and eagle
- "Like a sea of glass, clear as crystal"
- "Thunderings and voices and peals of thunder"

## The Woman and the Dragon

Revelation 12 describes:

- "A woman clothed with the sun, with the moon under her feet, and on her head a garland of twelve stars"
- "A great, fiery red dragon having seven heads and ten horns"
- "His tail drew a third of the stars of heaven and threw them to the earth"
- "A child was born who was to rule all nations with a rod of iron"

## Relevance to UFOs

John's vision describes:

- **Heavenly control center**: Throne room with seven lamps, sea of glass
- **Living creatures**: Similar to Ezekiel's chariot creatures
- **Celestial phenomena**: Woman clothed with sun, dragon with stars
- **Conflict**: The dragon and his angels warred against the child
- **Judgment**: Multiple plagues and judgments described

John's vision is one of the most detailed descriptions of a celestial encounter, and it connects to the modern UAP narrative of intelligent beings, advanced craft, and celestial phenomena.
