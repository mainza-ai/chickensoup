# Landauer

## Who

Rolf Landauer (1927–1999) was a German physicist who discovered that erasing information costs energy.

## Landauer's Principle

Erasing one bit of information costs at least $k_B T \ln 2$ of energy. This is the minimum energy cost of computation.

## The Connection to Maxwell's Demon

Landauer's principle resolves Maxwell's demon. The demon sorts molecules by reading information, but must erase its memory to continue operating. The thermodynamic debt is paid through memory erasure.

## The Connection to AI

AI is a Maxwell demon. It reads the world, builds internal models, and reduces local entropy. The cost of AI is the energy of erasing information.
