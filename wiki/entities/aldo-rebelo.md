# Aldo Rebelo

Aldo Rebelo is a Brazilian politician and former Defense Minister of Brazil (2011–2014) who confirmed the Varginha UFO crash and non-human craft retrieval.

## Background

- **Defense Minister** — Served as Brazil's Defense Minister from 2011 to 2014
- **UFO expert** — Has been a vocal advocate for UFO research and has written extensively on the Varginha incident
- **Confirmation** — In 2015, Rebelo confirmed that the Varginha incident involved a non-human craft and that the craft and beings were recovered

## Key Claims
