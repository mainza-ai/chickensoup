# Varginha UFO Crash

In 1996, the city of Varginha in Brazil experienced one of the most famous UFO incidents in modern history, with multiple witnesses reporting encounters with strange beings and a crashed craft.

## The Incident

On the night of March 24–25, 1996, dozens of residents of Varginha reported seeing three tall, slender beings with large eyes wandering the streets. The beings were described as having brownish skin and wearing what appeared to be uniforms. Multiple witnesses, including police officers and a local television journalist, reported seeing the beings.

## Key Witnesses

- **The three girls** — Three teenage girls (Leila, Daniela, and Janaina) were working at a video store when they encountered one of the beings
- **Police officers** — Two police officers (Ozires Silva and Jose Anibal) reported seeing the beings
- **Television journalist** — Local journalist Paulo Sanches reported seeing the beings
- **Dozens of residents** — Dozens of other residents reported seeing the beings

## The Crash and Retrieval

According to Aldo Rebelo, former Brazilian Defense Minister, the beings were recovered along with a craft after the incident. The retrieval was conducted by the Brazilian military and involved a specialized unit.

## Aldo Rebelo's Confirmation

In 2015, Aldo Rebelo, former Brazilian Defense Minister, confirmed that:
- The Varginha incident involved a non-human craft
- The craft and beings were recovered
- The retrieval was conducted by the Brazilian military
- The incident was kept secret for years

## Significance

- **Multiple witnesses** — Dozens of witnesses, including police officers and a journalist
- **Military involvement** — The Brazilian military was involved in the retrieval
- **Aldo Rebelo's confirmation** — The confirmation by a former Defense Minister adds significant weight to the incident
