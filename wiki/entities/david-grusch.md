# David Grusch

Former U.S. intelligence official who testified under oath before the House Intelligence Committee in 2023.

## Background

- 15+ years in intelligence, including work on UAP programs
- Served in multiple roles related to UAP intelligence
- Testified under oath in 2023

## Key Claims

- The U.S. has been running a secret UAP retrieval program for decades
- **Hundreds of craft** have been recovered
- **Non-human biologics** are being held (he calls it "the thing")
- The craft are **non-human in origin**
- The program is **multi-branch** (DIA, DARPA, NRO, CIA)
- The program is **well-funded** (black budget)
- The program is **active** (not just historical)

## Credibility

- Testified under oath before the House Intelligence Committee
- Has 15+ years of experience in UAP intelligence
- His claims are specific and consistent
- His claims are backed by other witnesses and evidence

## What's Uncertain

- Whether he personally saw the biologics (his claims are largely second-hand)
- Whether the craft are extraterrestrial or extraterrestrial
- The full extent of the program
- The location of the biologics
