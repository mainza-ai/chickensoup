# Mount Nyangani

Mount Nyangani is a mountain in Zimbabwe that former CIA remote viewer Lyn Buchanan claims is one of four alleged alien bases on Earth.

## Lyn Buchanan's Claims

- **Alien base** — Buchanan claims Mount Nyangani is an active alien base
- **Four bases** — Part of a network of four alien bases on Earth
- **Remote viewing** — Buchanan arrived at this conclusion through CIA remote viewing sessions
- **Connection to Ariel School** — The beings described at Ariel School (1994) are believed to be associated with the Mount Nyangani base

## Geographic Context

- Located in Zimbabwe, near Ruwa where the Ariel School incident occurred
- Zimbabwe has a long history of UFO sightings and encounters
- The region is rich in mineral deposits, which may be relevant to the alien presence

## Significance

- **Remote viewing evidence** — Buchanan's claims are based on CIA remote viewing, a technique that has been used to locate and describe distant objects
- **Connection to modern UFO lore** — Mount Nyangani connects Zimbabwe to the broader narrative of alien bases and UAP phenomena
- **The being connection** — The beings described at Ariel School may be the same beings associated with the Mount Nyangani base
