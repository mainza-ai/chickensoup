# Area 51

A highly classified U.S. Air Force facility in Nevada. Home of the U-2 spy plane development and a key location for UAP research.

## Features

- Located in the Nevada Test and Training Range
- Officially acknowledged by the CIA in 2013
- The "hedgy" — the perimeter fence with signs warning "Use of Deadly Force Authorized"
- Home of the U-2 spy plane development
- A key location for UAP research

## Connection to UAPs

- Claims that alien craft are brought to Area 51 for study
- Claims that S-4 (a sub-base) is where the craft are kept
- Claims that the craft are repaired and analyzed there
- Claims that the craft are used in UAP sightings

## What's Classified

- The full extent of UAP research
- The location and status of recovered craft
- The location of biologics
- The status of reverse-engineered craft
