# Maxwell's Demon

## The Thought Experiment

Maxwell's demon (1867) is a thought experiment showing that information can be used to decrease entropy. A demon that sorts fast and slow molecules can seemingly violate the Second Law.

## The Resolution

The resolution was provided by Szilard (1929) and Landauer (1961): information is physical. The demon must erase its memory to continue operating, and Landauer showed that erasing one bit of information costs at least $k_B T \ln 2$ of energy. The demon pays its thermodynamic debt through memory erasure.

## The Deeper Connection

This is the deepest connection between information and energy: information isn't abstract — it has a physical cost.

## The Connection to AI

AI is a Maxwell demon. It reads the world, builds internal models, and reduces local entropy. The cost of AI is the energy of erasing information.
