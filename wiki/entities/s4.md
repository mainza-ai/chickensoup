# S-4

S-4 is a sub-base of Area 51, located in the Nevada desert. It is the site where Bob Lazar claims to have worked on reverse-engineering alien craft.

## Bob Lazar's Claims

Bob Lazar claimed in 1989 that he worked at S-4 as a physicist, where he and a team of scientists reverse-engineered nine alien spacecraft that had been recovered in Roswell and other locations. According to Lazar:

- The craft use **Element 115** as a fuel source
- The craft generate a field that manipulates spacetime, allowing them to travel through space and time
- The craft can create "bubbles" in spacetime that allow them to travel at speeds far exceeding the speed of light
- The craft are powered by a reactor that uses Element 115 to create a field that manipulates spacetime

## Location

S-4 is located near Area 51 in the Nevada desert. It is a classified facility that is part of the larger Area 51 complex. The exact location is not publicly known, but it is believed to be in the Groom Lake area.

## Connection to UFO Retrieval Program

S-4 is part of the broader UFO retrieval program that has been operating since the 1930s. The facility is believed to be one of the primary sites where recovered UFOs and alien biologics are stored and studied.
