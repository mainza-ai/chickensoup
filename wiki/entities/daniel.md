# Daniel

## Who

Daniel was a Jewish prophet who lived during the Babylonian exile (c. 605-536 BC). He served in the courts of Babylonian kings and received multiple visions.

## The Throne Vision

Daniel 7 describes a vision of "a man" seated on a throne with fire and wheels of fire around it:

- "He sat on a throne of fiery flames, with wheels of burning fire"
- "A river of fire was flowing, coming out from before him"
- "Thousands attending Him, and ten thousand times ten thousand standing before Him"
- "The ancient of days" was seated, with white hair and robes
- "The court was seated, and the books were opened"

## The Glorious Man

Daniel 10 describes a more detailed encounter with a divine being:

- "Clothed in linen, whose waist was girded with gold of Uphaz"
- "Body like beryl"
- "Face like the appearance of lightning"
- "Eyes like torches of fire"
- "Arms and feet like burnished bronze"
- "Voice like the voice of a multitude"
- Daniel fell down, trembled, and heard a voice that strengthened him

## Relevance to UFOs

Daniel's visions describe:

- **Advanced technology**: "Wheels of burning fire," "river of fire"
- **Multiple witnesses**: "Thousands attending Him"
- **Judgment and observation**: "The books were opened"
- **Physical presence**: The being had a body, face, eyes, arms, feet
- **Communication**: Spoke to Daniel, strengthened him

Daniel's account is one of the most detailed descriptions of a divine being with advanced technology, and it connects to the modern UAP narrative of intelligent beings and advanced craft.
