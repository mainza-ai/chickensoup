# Bob Lazar

Claims to have worked on alien craft at S-4, a sub-base of Area 51, in the 1980s.

## Background

- Began case in 1989
- Claims to have worked at S-4 (a sub-base of Area 51)
- Claims to have worked on eight alien craft
- Claims to have worked on the propulsion system

## Key Claims

- **S-4** — A sub-base of Area 51 where alien craft are brought for repair and analysis
- **Element 115** — An exotic element (Moscovium) that produces gravity waves for propulsion
- **Eight craft** — He worked on eight alien craft
- **Propulsion** — The craft are propelled by gravity waves generated by Element 115
- **Bases on Earth** — At least 12 extraterrestrial bases on Earth, including one in Antarctica and one under the Pacific

## Consistency

- Has been remarkably consistent over 35+ years
- His claims have held up under scrutiny
- He has been interviewed extensively
- He has been on record in multiple media appearances

## Controversy

- Some details have been questioned (e.g., his education, his work at Los Alamos)
- His claims about Element 115 are scientifically questionable
- Some witnesses have disputed his account
- The military has not confirmed his account
