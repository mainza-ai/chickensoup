# The New Science of Unidentified Aerospace-Undersea Phenomena (UAP)

**Knuth, Ailleris, Agrama, et al. 2025. Progress in Aerospace Sciences. 195 pages.**

A comprehensive review of UAP research, covering government studies from 1933 to present, physical evidence, and the current scientific understanding of UAP.

## The Five Observables

1. **Positive lift without flight surfaces**
2. **Sudden/instantaneous acceleration**
3. **Hypersonic velocity without signatures**
4. **Trans-medium travel** (air, water, space)
5. **Low observability/cloaking**

## Key Topics

- Government studies from Scandinavia, US, France, Russia, China, Canada
- Physical evidence: crashes, collisions, deposits
- Scientific field studies: Project Hessdalen, Galileo Project, VASCO, SETI Kingsland
- Organizations: AIAA, SCU, NARCAP, SUAPS, SOL Foundation

## Relevance to Time Travel

The trans-medium travel and field manipulation observed in UAPs are consistent with the field-based propulsion mechanisms described in our time travel machinery architecture.
