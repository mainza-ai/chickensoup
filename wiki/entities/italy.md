# Italy

A country in southern Europe with a rich history of scientific and cultural achievement.

## UFO Connection

- **1933** — Magenta UFO crash recovered by Mussolini's regime
- **1937** — Vatican crash recovery (possibly related to or conflated with Magenta)
- Italy's central location in the Mediterranean and its scientific institutions make it a key location for UFO recovery

## Significance

Italy's role in the early UFO recovery program — with both the Magenta crash and the Vatican crash — supports the theory that the retrieval program began in the 1930s, possibly with multiple recoveries across Europe before being transferred to the United States.
