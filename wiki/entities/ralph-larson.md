# Ralph Moat Larson

## Who

Ralph Moat Larson is a former CIA officer (20+ years) and Department of Energy intelligence director. He served as Moscow station chief, worked alongside NSA Director Michael Hayden, CIA Director George Tenet, and briefed President George W. Bush, VP Dick Cheney, and UK PM Tony Blair.

## Time Travel Claim

In 1991, during a pilgrimage to Mount Athos, Greece, Larson claims he experienced time travel to the 14th century. He reports living as a monk in medieval Mount Athos for months — eating, walking, socializing, and sleeping in his physical body. He describes the experience as indistinguishable from his current waking reality — not remote viewing or astral projection, but full physical time travel.

He left his 20th century identity behind to live as a recluse on Mount Athos, burning his hut in the manner of a traditional hermit.

## UFO Knowledge

Given his DOE intelligence role (which he states handles more sensitive material than the CIA, including UFO crash retrieval programs), Larson was questioned on UAP topics in coordination with David Grusch and Robert Hastings. His answers on propulsion modalities, Roswell, and exotic materials are ambiguous but suggest insider knowledge of non-chemical propulsion.

## Significance

Larson is notable for being a high-ranking intelligence official making concrete, first-person time travel claims — not as theory but as personal experience. His case connects the intelligence community's relationship with fringe phenomena, mystical experience (Mount Athos, Virgin Mary apparitions), and the DOE's role in UAP secrecy.
