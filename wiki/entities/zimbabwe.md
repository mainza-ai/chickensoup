# Zimbabwe

A country in southern Africa with a rich history of UFO sightings and encounters.

## The Ariel School Incident

In September 1994, more than 60 schoolchildren at Ariel School in Ruwa reported seeing a silver craft land near their playground, along with small beings with large eyes. The children's accounts were remarkably consistent and have been investigated by researchers including Lyn Buchanan.

## Mount Nyangani

Mount Nyangani is a mountain in Zimbabwe that former CIA remote viewer Lyn Buchanan claims is one of four alien bases on Earth.

## Significance

Zimbabwe has a long history of UFO sightings and encounters, and is home to both the Ariel School incident and the Mount Nyangani alien base. The country's central location in southern Africa may be relevant to the broader narrative of UAP phenomena.
