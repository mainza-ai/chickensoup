# Boltzmann

## Who

Ludwig Boltzmann (1844–1906) was an Austrian physicist who founded statistical mechanics.

## The Equation

Boltzmann's equation — $S = k \log W$ — is engraved on his tombstone in Vienna. It connects entropy $S$ to the number of microstates $W$:

- $S$ = entropy
- $k$ = Boltzmann constant
- $W$ = number of microstates compatible with the macrostate

This is the foundation of statistical mechanics and the deepest insight about entropy.

## The Connection to Information

Boltzmann's equation is the bridge between thermodynamics and information theory. Shannon's entropy formula $H = -\sum p_i \log p_i$ is the same equation in a different form.
