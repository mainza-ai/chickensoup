# Exponential Quantum Speedup

**Babbush, Berry, Kothari, Somma, Wiebe. 2023. Physical Review X.**

The theoretical foundation for simulating the dynamics of coupled classical oscillators with exponential quantum speedup.

## Key Results

- Maps the oscillators' dynamics onto Schrödinger evolution
- Simulating N coupled oscillators scales at most polynomially in log(N)
- Exponential speedup over classical methods
- Uses Quantum Singular Value Transformation (QSVT) for time evolution

## Relevance to UAP

UAPs appear to manipulate fields and oscillators in spacetime. This paper provides the quantum algorithm for simulating those field dynamics — the same physics that may underlie UAP propulsion.
