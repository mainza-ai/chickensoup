# Mussolini

Benito Mussolini, fascist dictator of Italy from 1922 to 1943.

## UFO Connection

- **1933** — Mussolini's regime recovered a UFO that crashed in Magenta, Italy
- The craft was studied and preserved by the Italian government
- After Italy's fall in the war, the craft may have been transferred to the United States, entering the U.S. UFO retrieval program

## Significance

Mussolini's regime had significant resources and scientific institutions capable of studying and preserving rare objects. The Magenta crash recovery predates Roswell by 14 years and supports the theory that the UFO retrieval program began in the 1930s.
