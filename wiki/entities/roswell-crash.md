# Roswell Crash

The 1947 crash in Roswell, New Mexico, which may have been an alien craft.

## Key Facts

- 1947 — A mysterious craft crashed in New Mexico
- Military initially said "flying disc," then retracted to "weather balloon"
- Many believe it was an alien vessel
- The crash is the first major UAP recovery event
