# Nikola Tesla

Nikola Tesla (1856–1943) was a Serbian-American inventor, electrical engineer, and physicist whose work on wireless energy transmission, electromagnetism, and high-voltage electricity is widely believed to be connected to UAP propulsion and the broader UFO disclosure narrative.

## Key Inventions

- **Wireless energy transmission** — Tesla's Wardenclyffe Tower was designed to transmit energy wirelessly across the globe
- **The death ray (teleforce)** — A particle beam weapon that Tesla claimed could destroy aircraft at a distance
- **The magnifying transmitter** — A device that could transmit electrical energy without wires and with minimal loss
- **Radio** — Tesla held the original patents for radio, though Guglielmo Marconi is often credited
- **Alternating current (AC)** — Tesla's AC system is the basis for modern electrical power
- **Tesla coil** — A transformer that produces high-voltage, low-current, high-frequency alternating-current electricity

## Connection to UFOs

- **Signal from Mars** — In 1899, Tesla reported receiving signals from Mars, which he interpreted as evidence of intelligent life on the planet
- **Wardenclyffe Tower** — Tesla's tower is often cited as a precursor to UAP propulsion systems, as it demonstrated the ability to transmit energy wirelessly
- **Death ray** — Tesla's "teleforce" is seen as a precursor to modern UAP energy weapons
- **Wireless energy** — Tesla's work on wireless energy transmission is believed to be the basis for UAP propulsion systems

## The Mysterious Death

Tesla died on January 7, 1943, in the New Yorker Hotel room 3327 at age 86. His death was surrounded by mystery:

- **The FBI seizure** — Shortly after Tesla's death, the FBI seized his papers, including his notes on the death ray and other inventions
- **The missing papers** — Some of Tesla's most important papers are said to have been lost or destroyed
- **The timing** — Tesla's death occurred during World War II, a period of intense military activity and secret research
- **The connection to JFK** — Some researchers believe that Tesla's death was connected to the same forces that later killed JFK, as both were preparing to reveal classified information about UFOs and UAPs
